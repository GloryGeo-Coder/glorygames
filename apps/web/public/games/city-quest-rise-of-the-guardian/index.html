City Quest: Rise of the Guardian

When a mysterious gang disrupts life across the township, a young guardian rises to restore balance. Run across rooftops, help local characters, recover lost tokens, and face the rogue taxi boss.

Story mode Platform jumping NPC missions Boss stages Unlockable skins

How to Play

  • Reach the mentor or guide character to receive your mission.
  • Collect coins, taxi tokens, and badges while avoiding enemies and obstacles.
  • Use jump to clear gaps, taxis, crates, spikes, and moving platforms.
  • Use Shoot to fire guardian energy at walkers, drones, and bosses.
  • Flying drones can fire back, so dodge their energy bolts.
  • Collect shield, heart, and blaster power-ups to survive harder stages.
  • Finish a level to unlock the map and continue the story.
