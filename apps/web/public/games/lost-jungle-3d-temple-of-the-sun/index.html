How to Play

  • Move forward through the jungle path and collect Sun Relics.
  • Collect keys to open locked gates.
  • Step on glowing pressure plates to unlock hidden bridges.
  • Use Jump, Dash, and Torch powers to survive traps and enemies.
  • Dodge snakes, spiders, bats, stone monkeys, rolling boulders, spikes, and darts.
  • Defeat boss guardians at key temple stages.
